About Me

Grounded in connection,
guided by compassion

I believe every therapeutic relationship is unique, and I do not take for granted the trust it takes for someone to share their inner world with another person.

I grew up in a culture that values "Yuanfen" (緣分), a concept that reflects the meaningful connections and encounters we experience throughout life. This perspective deeply shapes how I approach therapy. It is an honor to accompany individuals through periods of healing, self-discovery, and growth.

In therapy, I strive to create a space that feels safe, collaborative, and affirming. I view people within the broader context of their lived experiences, including the cultural, relational, systemic, and intersectional factors that shape how they understand themselves and navigate the world.

I believe individuals are the experts of their own lives. Rather than positioning myself as someone who "has the answers," I work collaboratively with clients to explore patterns, deepen self-understanding, and identify meaningful directions for change. My therapeutic style is grounded in curiosity, compassion, authenticity, and respect for each person's pace and experiences.

"I also value the connection between the mind and body. Sometimes emotions and experiences are carried not only through thoughts, but also physically within the body. Creativity and body-based approaches can offer additional pathways toward understanding and healing."

我认为每一段关系、每一次相遇，都值得被珍惜，而不应被视为理所当然。

"相逢即是有缘"这句话，是我在与人相处时一直很努力践行的一个信念。走进咨询室，把自己的内在世界分享给另一个人，本身就不是一件容易的事。有时候，它甚至违背了我们在受伤或感到脆弱时，想把自己藏起来的本能。而能够被允许陪伴一个人走过疗愈、自我理解与成长的过程，对我而言一直都是一件很珍贵的事情。

在咨询过程中，我会尝试与来访者一起建立一个安全、能够自在做自己的空间。我们每个人的家庭、关系、社会环境、文化背景，以及不同的身份经验，都会影响我们如何理解自己，以及如何与他人相处。

在咨询室里，我相信来访者比任何人都更了解自己的生命，以及自己真正想前往的方向。我也相信，只有来访者最清楚事情背后的脉络，以及为什么有些在外人眼中看似直接的答案，对自己而言却未必是最适合的。因此，我希望能够提供一个安全的空间，与来访者一起探索与整理内在经验，理解过去的经历如何塑造了现在的情感、关系与行为模式，并在这样的理解之中，慢慢产生疗愈与改变。

「我也认为身体和心灵会彼此影响。很多经验并不只是停留在脑海里，也会被身体记住。在适合的情况下，我倾向将身体觉察、接地、正念，以及其他体验式方法融入治疗中，协助来访者更理解自己的内在经验与情绪。」
